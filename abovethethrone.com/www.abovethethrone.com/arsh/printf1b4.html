Wahb bin Jareer (d. 206H): The Jahmiyyah Are Trying to Say There Is Nothing Above the Heaven
Friday, October 02 2009 - by Abu Iyaad
Read more articles at AboveTheThrone.Com

Muhammad bin Hammaad said:

I heard Wahb bin Jareer saying: "Beware of the opinion of Jahm, for they are trying (to say) that there is nothing above the Heaven, and this is nothing but the inspiration of Iblees, it is nothing but kufr".

Source: Adh-Dhahabi in "Mukhtasar al-Uluww" (p. 170).

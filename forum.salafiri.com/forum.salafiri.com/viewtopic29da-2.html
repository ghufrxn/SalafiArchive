Time of Remembering Allaah Morning and Evening – Shaykh al-Albaani [1420H]

Question: “When is the time for the adhkaar[ words of remembrance of Allaah ] of the morning and evening?”

Shaykh al-Albaani (rahimahullaah) answers:

“The adhkaar of the evening are (made) after ‘asr, and the adhkaar of the morning are (made) after fajr.”

Source: silsilat ul-hudaa wa nnoor – the series of guidance and light – tape no. 192

Books Recommended by Shaykh Ahmed an-Najmee in Tawheed & ‘Aqeedah

Question:

What are the books that you advise studying in the realm of Tawheed and ‘Aqeedah?

Answer:

The first of them is ‘Kitaab at-Tawheed’ and ‘Usool uth-Thalaatah’ and ‘al-Qawaaid al-‘Arba’a’, ‘Kashfu Shubuhaat’ and the books of Shaykh ul-Islaam ibn Taymiyyah upon the subject of the Names and Attributes (of Allaah). The books of Shaykh Muhammad bin Abdul Wahaab and his children and grandchildren and students. Then those (books) after them, like ‘Tawheed’ of ibn Khuzaymah and ‘ar-Rad ‘alal-Jahmiyyah’ of ‘Uthmaan bin Sa’eed ad-Darimee, ‘al-Ibaanahtul Kubraa’ and ‘(al-Ibaanahtu) Sughraa’ of ibn Battah, ‘Sharh Usool I’tiqaad Ahlus Sunnah’ by Laalikaaee, and (there are) many books other than them.

Taken From Daarul Minhaaj, p.182.
Shaykh Ahmad bin Yahyaa an-Najmee

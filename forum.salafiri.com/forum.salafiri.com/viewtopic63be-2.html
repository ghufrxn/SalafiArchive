Following The Hadīth- Ibn Sīrīn (Died 110 A.H)

Posted: Sat Feb 18, 2017 10:23 pm
by Abu Ubaydah
Ibn Sīrīn said,

"People were of the view that so long as a person is on the path of Hadīth(T.N. i.e by following and acting upon them) then he is on the correct path."

(It's chain is authentic. Jamiʾ Bayān al-ʾIlm Wa Fadhlīhī of Ibn ʿAbdul Bar narration no.1778, Compendium Explaining The Nature Of Knowledge, Sunan ad-Dārimi, al-Muqadimah no.142)

Translated by Abu Ubaydah

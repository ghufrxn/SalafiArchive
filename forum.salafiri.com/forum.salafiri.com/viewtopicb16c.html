From that which is specific to al-Uluhiyyah:
The sujood, so those who prostrate for other than Him (Allaah) indeed have made comparison with Him And from it: at-Tawakil, so those who trust upon other than Him indeed made comparison with Him.

And from it: at-Tawbah, so those who repent to other than him indeed made comparison with Him.

And from it: the swearing with His (Great) Name; so those who swear by other than Him indeed made comparison with Him. And from it: the slaughtering for Him, so those who slaughter for other than Him indeed made comparison with Him.

Translated by the poor servant of Allaah in most need of His Mercy and Forgiveness.

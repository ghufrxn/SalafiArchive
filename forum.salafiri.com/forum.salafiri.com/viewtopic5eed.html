Zamzam Water and the Answered Supplication – Shaykh al-Albaani

Posted: Thu Oct 06, 2016 10:28 pm
by Shehzad Sattar
Question #13: “The du’aa (supplication) is answered when drinking zamzam water, so is this particularly in Makkah or in any place?”

Shaykh al-Albaani (rahimahullaah) answers:

“The first one (i.e., particularly in Makkah) is that which I consider to be correct.”

Taken from silsilat ul-hudaa wa nnoor – the series of guidance and light – tape no. 814

Allāh will grant whoever recites this seven times in the morning or evening whatever he desires from this world or the next.

ﺣَﺴْﺒِﻲَ ﺍﻟﻠﻪُ ﻟَﺂ ﺇِﻟَﻪَ ﺇِﻟَّﺎ ﻫُﻮَ ﻋَﻠَﻴْﻪِ ﺗَﻮَﻛَّﻠْﺖُ ﻭَﻫُﻮَ ﺭَﺏُّ ﺍﻟْﻌَﺮْﺵِ ﺍﻟْﻌَﻈِﻴﻢِ

Hasbiyallaahu laa 'ilaaha 'illaa Huwa 'alayhi tawakkaltu wa Huwa Rabbul-'Arshil-'Adheem

"Allāh is sufficient for me. There is none worthy of worship but Him. I have placed my trust in Him. He is Lord of the Majestic Throne."

(Recite seven times in Arabic)

Ibn As-Sunni (no. 71), Abu Dawud 4/321. Sahih Hadith.

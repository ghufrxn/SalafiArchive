Traits of the Salafi Manhaj

Posted: Sat Mar 25, 2017 8:30 pm
by Abu Shaqran Zahiri
(1) – 1st Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Dawah is for Allah Alone
(2) – 2nd Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – The Priorities of the Manhaj of Dawah must be clear to the Da’ee
(3) – 3rd Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Adhering to Ittiba (obedience) and refraining from Ibtida (innovation)
(4) – 4th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Tarbiyah with ‘ilm (knowledge)
(5) – 5th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Giving Importance to The Arabic Language
(6) – 6th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Academic Discipline
The Da’ee must be confident that he is academically well-acquainted with what he is calling to.
The Da’ee must know the Akhlaaq (praiseworthy manners) of a Da’ee
The Da’ee must have Prudence in handling the affairs of the people
(7) 7th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Callers to Jamaa’ah (unity) and to abandonment of disunity
(8) – 8th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Moderation: (al-wasatiyyah and al-a’tidal)
(9) – 9th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Salafi Manhaj is founded upon the fundamentals derived from the Sharee’ah texts
Explaining the fundamental, ‘Considering the wider MaslaHa over the restricted MaslaHa’
Explaining the Fundamental, ‘Blocking the Means (that lead to evil)’
(10) – 10th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – A Da’ee should spread Optimism
(11) – 11th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Refuting the doubts (that are raised) in the Deen
(12) – 12th Distinctive Trait of the Salafi Manhaj in Dawah – Guarding the Tongue from slandering the scholars

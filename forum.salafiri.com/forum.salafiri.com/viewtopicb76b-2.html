For Verily He Has Never Been A Scholar

Posted: Thu Dec 29, 2016 12:19 am
by Shehzad Sattar
A statement from Ibn Taymiyyah (May Allah have Mercy upon him)

The scholar knows the ignorant one, for verily he was once ignorant, and the ignorant one does not know the scholar, for verily he has never been a scholar.

Taken from Jawab I'tiradhaat Al-Misriyyah.
Translated by Hussain Suleman Abdul Sattar.

Refuting is from the Principles of Salafiyyah - Shaikh Sulayman al-Ruhaiyli

Posted: Wed Aug 24, 2016 12:36 am
by SRI



From 45:11

Some people claim to be upon Salafiyyah and they say we are not people of refutations! This opposes a principle from amongst the principles of Salafiyyah. Refutations of the people of innovation are from the principles of Salafiyyah. However as I have already mentioned, not all of us have to refute. However the point of discussion here is the principle (of refuting the people of innovation).

It is possible that a person may have an opinion about Sulayman's refutation (of an individual) and that Sulayman erred in his refutation, then this is something which is knowledge based and there is no problem with this. The problem however is with the one who claims Salafiyyah for himself, but rejects this principle (to refute the people of innovation), in which the Sunnah was preserved by.

Translated: Abu Maryam Abdul Qadir

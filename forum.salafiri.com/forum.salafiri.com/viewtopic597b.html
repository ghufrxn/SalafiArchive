What is Salafiyyah? - ash-Shaykh al-'Allãmah Sãlih al-Fawzãn

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Bestower of Mercy

This is a translation of a question posed to ash-Shaykh al-'Allãmah Sãlih al-Fawzãn may Allah preserve him.

The questioner says, “May Allah be good to you, what is Salafiyyah? Is it a hizb (group)? And what is the ruling of ascribing [oneself] to it?

Shaykh Sãlih al-Fawzãn may Allah preserve him replies, “Salafiyyah is the origin. [It is] the methodology of the Pious Predecessors from the Mahājireen and the Ansār and those who followed them in goodness. This is the origin. Who ever opposes and leaves it, he is misguided. This Ummah will split into seventy-three sects. All of them will be in the Hell-Fire except one. They [the Companions] said, “Who are they?” He (ﷺ) said,

((Those who are upon what I am upon today and my Companions.))

This is Salafiyyah. [It is] what the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) and his Companions were upon including those Imāms that came after them. This is Salafiyyah. It is not as they say that Salafiyyah is a sect from among the sects. No! It is the origin. The sects are those who have split off from it.”

Translated by Ismaeel Beaumont
Al Madinah an-Nabawwiyyah
The Prophet’s City (ﷺ)
19th Jumāda al-Awwal, 1436

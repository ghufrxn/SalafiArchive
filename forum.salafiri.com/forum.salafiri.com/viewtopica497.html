Imaam Bin Baaz (rahimahullah) on the Prophet’s Prayer Described by Imaam Al Albaanee (rahimahullah)

Posted: Thu Oct 20, 2016 11:19 pm
by Shehzad Sattar
Q: What is your Excellency’s opinion about the book the “Prophet’s Prayer Described” by Shaikh Muhammad Nasrud Deen Al Albaanee?

A: This is a good and beneficial book. Shaikh Muhammad Nasrud Deen Al Albaanee is a noble man and an ‘Allaamah. He has a great striving to revive the Sunnah and clarify that which is authentic from that which is unauthentic. He is thanked for his hard work may Allah reward him with good and multiply his reward. (However, with that remember), everyone makes mistakes and no one is free from error.

Author: Imaam Bin Baaz
Translator: Abu 'Abdis Salaam Al Juyaanee
Taken from Fataawa Noor ‘Alal Darb 121/25

If one of you dozes off...

Posted: Sun Mar 05, 2017 1:26 am
by Abu Faatimah
Abdillāh bin Umar رصي الله عنهما narrated, The Messenger of Allāh صلى الله عليه وسلم said:

If one of you dozes in the masjid on the day of Jumu'ah (Friday), he should move his position to another place.

Abee Dawūd, at-Tirmidhi and Authenticated by Shaykh al-Albāni (Saheeh al-Jāmi' 809)

[16] Question: There is a book called Mi'raaj Ibn 'Abbaas. Is it correct to attribute it to Ibn 'Abbaas, radyAllaahu 'anhumaa?

Answer: It is not correct to attribute this book to Ibn 'Abbaas, radyAllaahu 'anhumaa. I have spoken about Mi'raaj Ibn 'Abbaas in my refutation of Doctor Al-Bootee, which is circulated under the title: "Defending the Prophetic Hadeeth and Biography."

A person will not be a Muslim except by...

The great scholar ‘Abdurrahman Ibn Hassan Ibn Muhammad Ibn ‘Abd-el-Wahhab, may Allah have mercy on all of them, said:

"A person will not be a Muslim except by...

    - stripping himself off completely from major shirk,
    - disassociating himself from it (i.e. major shirk) and those who commit it,
    - and hating and opposing them to the best of his ability and strength.”

Translated by 'Abdurrahīm Ibn Muhammad Al-Hadhramī.
​Published on Salafi-Dawah.com.
​[Ad-Durar-us-Sanniyyah 545/11]

Should we Busy the People with “Where is Allaah” & other Matters of Belief?
Shaykh Ibn Baaz Rahimahullaah

Question: Some people say, “It is not correct to busy the general folk with affirmation of the matters of 'Aqeedah (Belief) such as the Highness of Allaah above the creation (al-‘Uluww) and other such matters”?

Answer: The ‘Aqeedah is the most important of affairs. The Prophet (sallallaahu alayhi wasallam) was in Makkah for thirteen years, and all of that time was given to the 'Aqeedah. The 'Aqeedah is the foundation of the Religion.

Sharh Kashfish-Shubuhaat of Imaam Muhammad ibn Abdul-Wahhaab, by Shaikh Abdul-Azeez Ibn Baaz (rahimahullaah), p. 38 (Daarul-Minhaaj).

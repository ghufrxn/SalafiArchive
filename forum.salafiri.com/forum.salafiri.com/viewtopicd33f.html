The Two Of Them Are In The Fire And Only One Will Be Rewarded With Jannah

Posted: Wed Sep 21, 2016 12:02 am
by Shehzad Sattar
The two of them are in the fire and only one will be rewarded with Jannah

The ulama of ahlus sunnah wal jammah have established that people are divided into three types; out of whom two are from the fire and only one will enter jannah.

1. The Mustakbir, it is the one who is arrogant and denies the existence of Allah تعالى and lives his life following his desires. He is from the people of the fire on the Day of Judgement.

2. The Mushrik, he does not deny the existence of Allah تعالى but associates patners with Allah and thus seeks the anger of Allah تعالى by giving Allah's right of worship, his lordship and qualities and attributes to other than Allah. He is also from Ahlul Naar, the people of the fire.

3. The Mustaslim or Muslim, this is the one upon haq. He denies the existence of any deity being worthy of worship, makes kufr of taghoot and is firm upon the tawheed of Allah. On the Day of Judgement he will be the only one to enter Jannah.

From the notes of Abu Ishaq Jalal Zulfiqar taken from Sharh Sareeh as-Sunnah of ash-Shaykh Abdul Aziz al-Raajihi (حفظه الله)

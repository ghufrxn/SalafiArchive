The Shi’ah Papers Part 1 – Ali (RadhiAllahu Anhu) Rebuking The Shi’ah Rafidah and Nasibi Extremists.

Compiled, Translated and Annotated
Abu Hibban & Abu Khuzaimah Ansaari

Ali (Radhiallaahu Anhu) said,

“A nation (a group of people) who love me will enter the Hell fire due to their (blind extreme) love of me, similarly a nation having animosity towards me will enter the Hell fire due to their animosity.”

(Kitaab Fadhaa’il as-Sahaabah 2/565 no.952 Shaikh Zubair Ali Za’i said, Its chain is authentic, Kitaab as-Sunnah no.983 of Ibn Abee Aasim, Shaikh Zubair Alee authenticated it.)

Ali (Radhiallaahu Anhu) also said,

“Two men (types of ie groups) will be destroyed concerning me, one who is extreme in exaggeration (in love of me) and the other who is extreme in his animosity towards me.”

(Fadhaa’il as-Sahaabah 2/571 no.964 Shaikh Zubair Ali Za’i said, Its chain is authentic)

THE BEST PRAYER - Fajr On Friday in Congregation.

The Prophet (peace and blessings be upon him) said,

”The best prayer with Allaah is Fajr on Friday in congregation.”

[Collected by Al-Bayhaqi in Imam and Abu Nuaym in Hilyah . Shaykh Al-Albani (may Allaah have mercy on him) graded this hadeeth as being authentic in Silsilatu Saheehah 4/91]

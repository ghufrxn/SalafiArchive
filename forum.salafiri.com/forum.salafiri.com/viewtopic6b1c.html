Perfect Your Worship of Allaah Wherever You Are


*AS-SAHEEHAH (no. 3146):
ad – Doolaabee reported in „al – Kunaa‟ (1/179-180) from a hadeeth of Aboo
Hurairah - radiyallaahu ‟anhu - who said:
“A group of desert – dwellers came to Allaah‟s Messenger  and said: „O
Messenger of Allaah! Some companions of the Qur·aan claim that no actions
benefit without migration (Hijrah) and fighting jihaad in Allaah‟s cause.‟
So Allaah‟s Messenger  said:
<<Wherever you are and you perfect your worship of Allaah – then receive the
good news of Paradise>>.
*Shaikh al – Albaanee said: “This is a „hasan‟ chain of narration…”
***
[Translated by Aboo Talhah Daawood ibn Ronald Burbank - rahimahullaah]

The seekers of two concerns are never satisfied...

Posted: Sun Jan 08, 2017 2:31 pm
by Abu Faatimah
قَالَ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ

مَنومَانِ لا يَشْبَعَانِ:

طَالِبُ عِلْمٍ وطَالِبُ دُّنْيَا

The seekers of two concerns are never satisfied:

1. The seeker of Knowledge

2. The seeker of the Dunyah.

Saheeh al-Jāmi' 6624 - Shaykh Muhammad Nāsir ad-Deen al-Albāni رحمه الله

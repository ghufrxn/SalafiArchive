What Is The Best Book Related To Tawheed (Monotheism) And The Islamic ‘Aqeedah (Creed) And How Can It Be Obtained?

Posted: Sat Sep 03, 2016 2:28 am
by Shehzad Sattar
What is the best book related to Tawheed (monotheism) and the Islamic ‘Aqeedah (creed) and how can it be obtained?

The scholars of the Standing Committee for Issuing Verdicts were asked:

What is the best book related to Tawheed (monotheism) and the Islamic ‘Aqeedah (creed) and how can it be obtained?

They replied:

The greatest book and the best book that clarifies the correct ‘Aqeedah (creed) is the Book of Allaah, Glorified and Exalted is He, then the Ahaadeeth of His Messenger (blessings and peace of Allaah be upon him), and from among the best books in that regard is Fath al-Majeed; al-‘Aqeedah al-Waasitiyyah; al-‘Uluww li’l ‘Aliyy al-Ghaffaar; al-Tawassul wa’l Waseelah; Mukhtasar al-Sawaa‘iq al-Mursalah; Tatheer al-I’tiqaad and Sharh al-Tahhaawiyyah. End quote.

Standing Committee for Academic Research and Issuing Verdicts

Shaykh ‘Abd al-‘Azeez ibn Baaz, Shaykh ‘Abd al-Razzaaq ‘Afeefi, Shaykh ‘Abd-Allaah ibn Ghudayaan, Shaykh ‘Abd-Allaah ibn Qa’ood.

Fataawaa al-Lajnah al-Daa’imah (2/178)

Brief Biography of Shaykh Sālim bin Sa‘d aṭ-Ṭawīl

Posted: Tue Aug 23, 2016 10:17 pm
by SRI
Shaykh Sālim bin Sa‘d aṭ-Ṭawīl (Ḥafiẓahullāh)
o The Shaykh is Abū Sa‘d Sālim bin Sa‘d bin Sālim aṭ-Ṭawīl.

o The Shaykh was born May 16th, 1962.

o The Shaykh graduated in 1407H, from the department of Uṣūl-ud-Dīn, at Imām Muḥammad bin Sa‘ūd Islamic University in Qaṣīm, in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

o The Shaykh is an Imām and Khaṭīb under the Ministry of Islamic Affairs in Kuwait, since 1991CE.

o From 1402-1407H, The Shaykh was a regular student of al-‘Allāmah Muḥammad bin Ṣāliḥ al-‘Uthaymīn, may Allāh have mercy upon him. Also, he has spent a number of years close to the Shaykh of Salafiyyīn in Kuwait Abū Yūsuf ‘Abdur-Raḥmān ‘Abduṣ-Ṣamad, may Allāh have mercy upon him, as well as the Esteemed Shaykh Falaah bin Ismaa‘eel Mundakaar, may Allāh preserve him, and others.

o In 1995 CE, the Shaykh obtained a High Diploma in al-Ḥadīth ash-Sharīf from the College of Sharī‘ah at Kuwait University.

o In 2004 CE, the Shaykh obtained a Masters from the department of Ḥadīth and its Sciences at the University of Karachi, Egypt branch.

o The Shaykh is a Family Affairs Consultant under the Ministry of Justice in Kuwait.

o During the past twenty three years, the Shaykh has been teaching in Kuwait and other countries, like United Arab Emirates (UAE), Yemen, the United Kingdom, the United States of America, and others.

o Here are some of his current classes:
1. Explanation of al-Wajīz fī Fiqh as-Sunnah wal-Kitāb al-‘Azīz.

2. Explanation of Sharḥ al-‘Aqīdah al-Wāsiṭiyah by al-‘Allāmah Muḥammad bin Ṣāliḥ al-‘Uthaymīn, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

3. Explanation of al-‘Iddah Sharḥ al-‘Umdah fī Fiqh Imām-us-Sunnah Aḥmad bin Ḥanbal, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

4. Explanation of Muftāḥ Dār as-Sa‘ādah.

5. Explanation of Sharḥ Ḥilyah Ṭālib al-‘Ilm by al-‘Allāmah Muḥammad bin Ṣāliḥ al-‘Uthaymīn, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

6. Explanation of Lum’at-ul-I’tiqād by al-‘Allāmah Muḥammad bin Ṣāliḥ al-‘Uthaymīn, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

7. Explanation of Taysīr al-‘Ullām Sharḥ ‘Umtadah al-Aḥkām by al-‘Allāmah ‘Abdullāh Āl Bassām, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

8. Explanation of Sharḥ Ḥadīth Jibrīl fī Ta’līm ad-Dīn by al-‘Allāmah ‘Abdul-Muḥsin al-‘Abbād, may Allāh preserve him.

9. Explanation of Buhjat Qlūb al-Abrār wa Qurrat ‘Uyūn al-Akhyār fī Sharḥi Jawāmi’ al-Akhbār by al-‘Allāmah ‘Abdur-Raḥmān bin Nāṣir as-Sa‘dī, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

10. Explanation of al-Qawl al-Mufīd Sharḥ Kitāb at-Tawḥīd by al-‘Allāmah Muḥammad bin Ṣāliḥ al-‘Uthaymīn, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

11. Explanation of Kitāb al-Jāmi‘ fī al-Ādāb min Bulūgh al-Marām.

12. Explanation of Sharḥ Nawāqiḍ al-Islām lil-Imām Muḥammad bin ‘Abdul-Wahhāb, may Allāh have mercy upon him, by al-‘Allāmah Ṣāliḥ al-Fawzān, may Allāh preserve him.

13. Explanation of ad-Durūs al-Muhimmah li-‘Āmmat-il-Ummah lil-Imām ‘Abdul-‘Azīz bin ‘Abdullāh bin Bāz, may Allāh have mercy upon him.

Who is like you, O son of Adam?

Posted: Sun Dec 25, 2016 1:14 pm
by Abu Faatimah
Abu Bakr bin Abdullah al Muzani رحمه الله said:

Who is like you, O son of Adam? Whenever you wish, you use water to make Ablution, go to the place for worship and thus enter the presence of your Lord (i.e. start praying) without a translator/barrier or a barrier between you and Him!

Al Bidayah Wan-Nihayah 9/256

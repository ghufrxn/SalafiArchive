Using The Sunnah (Hadīth) To Answer Doubts - ʿUmar Bin al-Khattāb (Raḍī Allāhū ʿAnhū) (Died 23 A.H)

Posted: Sat Feb 18, 2017 10:16 pm
by Abu Ubaydah
ʿUmar Bin al-Khattāb (Raḍī Allāhū ʿAnhū) said,

"Without a shadow of a doubt in the near future there will come a people who will argue against you with the (apparent) unclear parts of the Qurʾān. So you should reply to them with the Sunnah (Hadīth) because the people of the Sunnah know the Book of Allah (Qurʾān) the most."

(This narration is Mawqūf Sahīh. Jamiʾ Bayān al-ʾIlm Wa Fadhlīhī of Ibn ʿAbdul Bar narration no.1701, Compendium Explaining The Nature Of Knowledge, Sunan ad-Dārimi, al-Muqadimah no.121)

Translated by Abu Ubaydah

“Paradise has one hundred degrees

Posted: Tue Feb 21, 2017 8:59 am
by Abu Faatimah
FB_IMG_1487661916972.jpg (201.1 KiB) Viewed 20800 times
"إن في الجنة مائة درجة أعدها الله للمجاهدين في سبيل الله ما بين الدرجتين كما بين السماء والأرض فإذا سألتم الله فاسألوه الفردوس، فإنه أوسط الجنة، وأعلى الجنة، فوقه عرش الرحمن."

“Paradise has one hundred degrees which Allāh has prepared for those who strive in jihād for the sake of Allāh. The distance between each two degrees is like the distance between the Heavens and the Earth. When you ask of Allāh, ask Him for al-Firdaws, for it is in the middle of Paradise and is the highest part of Paradise, and above it is the Throne of the Most Merciful.”

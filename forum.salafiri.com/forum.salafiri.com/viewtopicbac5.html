In Defence of Shaikh al-Islam Ibn Taymiyyah and Refuting Shaikh Taqi al-Subki

Posted: Tue Aug 23, 2016 7:10 pm
by AbuKhuzaimahAnsari
The following are two poems in defence of Shaikh al-Islam ibn Taymiyyah titled

al-Himayah al-Islamiyyah Fi al-Intisar Li-Madhab Ibn Taymiyyah
of
Abi Muzaffar Yusuf bin Muhammad al-E'badi al-Surramarri [696 - 776H]

and

Qasidah Fi al-Radd A'la al-Taqi al-Subki Wa'l Dif'ah An Ibn Taymiyyah
of
Abi abdullah Muhammad bin Yusuf l-Yafʾī al-Shāfʾī al-Yamni

with the verification and notes by Shaikh Salah al-Din Maqbul Ahmad

pp.105

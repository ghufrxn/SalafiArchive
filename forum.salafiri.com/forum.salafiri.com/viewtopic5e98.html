Condition of Ibn Sireen’s Book About Dreams – Shaykh Muhammad Nasiruddin al-Albani

Posted: Sat Sep 17, 2016 12:21 am
by Shehzad Sattar
Shaykh al-Albaani was asked the following question:

Question:

السؤال :
عندي كتاب منسوب لابن سيرين ؟

I have a book ascribed to Ibn Sireen (so what do you say regarding it)?

Answer:

الجواب :
لابن سيرين في المنامات ؟ حسبك أنه كتاب منامات ، لا يعتمد عليه

Ibn Sireen about dreams ? It is sufficient for you to know that the Book on dreams is not relied upon”.

[Silsilatul Huda wan-Nur no. 93]

Translated by

Faisal Ibn Abdul Qaadir Ibn Hassan
Abu Sulaymaan

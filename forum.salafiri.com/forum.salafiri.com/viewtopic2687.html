Revise Your Knowledge - Al-Awzaa’ee

Posted: Thu Aug 25, 2016 5:52 pm
by Shehzad Sattar
Part of seeking knowledge is to REVIEW IT! How many times have we heard people say they are a haafidh but forgot many parts of it due to negligence, laziness, and leaving off revision??

Al-Awzaa’ee narrates that Ibn Shihaab az-Zuhree 124 AH said,

إنما يُذهب العلم نسيان ،بترك المذاكرة .

”Knowledge is lost through forgetfulness and abandoning revision”

Taken from: Jaami’ul Bayaan al-‘ilm wa fadhlihi volume 1 page 213

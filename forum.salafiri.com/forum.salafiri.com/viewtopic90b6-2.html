Do Not Criticize Adam (Peace Be Upon Him)
By Shaykh Fawzan

Question: What is the ruling on the one who says: If Adam peace be upon him, had not eaten from the tree, we would be in paradise. Is there anything wrong with this statement?

Shaykh Fawzan: This is not permissible, because this is equates to belittling Adam peace be upon him. And Adam repented to Allah and Allah accepted his repentance; thus this speech is not permissible. Be concerned with yourself oh my brother. No one is going to enter paradise without actions. You will not enter paradise without actions my brother.

Translated by Rasheed ibn Estes Barbee

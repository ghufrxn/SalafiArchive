The Best Books on Tawheed - The Permanent Committee for Islaamic Research & Verdicts

Reference: Fataawaa Al Lajnah Ad Daaimah, question no.3778, Vol. 3, P.250, 3rd edition, 1999 CE.

Question: What is the best book for studying Tawheed and the Islaamic ‘Aqeedah and how can a person obtain it?

Response: The greatest and best book that clarifies the correct ‘Aqeedah is the book of Allaah, then the narrations of His messengerصلى الله عليه وسلم .

The best books in the subject of ‘Aqeedah are the books:

Fath Al Majeed;
Al ‘Aqeedah Al Wasitiyah;
Al ‘Uloo Lil ‘Ali AlGhafaar;
At Tawasul wal Waseelah;
Mukhtasar Al Sawaiq Al Mursalah;
Tatheer Al ‘Itiqaad;
Sharh Al Tahaweeyah.

…and Allaah is the Granter of success.

مجموع الفتاوى ٣٥/ ١٤٩

Shaykh ul Islām Ibn Taymiyyah (رحمه الله) said:

These people who are known by the name An-Nusayriyyah and all other forms of Al-Qaraamitah al-Baatniyah (deviant Batni sects of the past) are worse disbelievers than the Jews and Christians. It would be rather more appropriate to say that they were worse disbelievers than even majority of polytheists.
Their harm upon the Ummah of Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم is far greater than those disbelievers who are in continuous war against Muslims, like Tatars, Westerners and others.

Majmoo al-Fataawa 35/149

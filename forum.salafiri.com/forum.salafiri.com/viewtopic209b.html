1. Register on the forum. Use appropriate names. This is applicable to anyone who wants to contribute, discuss, share and learn.

memberlist.php?mode=contactadmin

This is to ensure i. safety, ii. accountability.

Referees could include current registrants or members of the forum, du'at or other socially active Muslims we can contact to verify. Registrants must specify the COUNTRY and CITY they reside in.

Registration WILL NOT be approved without the referees and the required information.

The Superiority of Praying The Voluntary Prayers In The House

The Prophet صلّى الله عليه و سلّم said: "The voluntary prayer of a man (in a place) where no person can see him equal to twenty-five times his prayer where people can see him."

Recorded by Musnad Abu Ya’ala, Hadeeth 3821. Shaykh Al-Albânî graded this narration as Sahih.

The Prophet صلّى الله عليه و سلّم said: "The superiority of the prayer of a man in his house over his prayer (in a place) so that people can see him, is like the superiority of the obligatory prayer over the voluntary."

Saheeh At-Targeeb Wa At-Tarheeb, Hadeeth 438. Shaykh Al-Albânî graded this narration as Hasan.

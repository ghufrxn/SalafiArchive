A Beautiful Quote By Imam ash-Shafiee

Mahmud Shukri al Alusi said:
أما سمعت قول الإمام الشافعي رضي الله عنه حيث قال:
....لسانك لا تذكر به عورة امرىء ... فكلك عورات وللناس ألسن
وعينك إن أرتك يوما نقيصة ... لناس فقل يا عين للناس أعين...


Haven't you heard the saying of Imam ash-Shafiee May Allah be pleased with him, who said:

“Do not expose the sins of another person with your tongue, because people have tongues also who can expose you. If you see any fault in others, then say to your eye "O eye people also have eyes" Who can see your faults.”


[Ghayat al Amani 2/33]

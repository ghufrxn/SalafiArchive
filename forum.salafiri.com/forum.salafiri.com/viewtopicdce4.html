Everyone Will Know Muhammad Is The Best Of Mankind

In the Name of Allah the Most Gracious the Most Merciful

Allah the Exalted said:

    "It may be that your Lord will raise you to a station of praise and glory."


Imam Sadi—may Allah have mercy upon him—said about this verse:

He shall receive this station of praise, and it is the station where he will be praised by all of creation, the first of them and the last of them. And this is during the greatest intercession when the creation will go to Adam for him to intercede on their behalf, then they will go to Nuh, then to Ibrahim, then to Musa, and then to Jesus. All of them will make an excuse and defer the responsibility. Until they go to the best of mankind for him to intercede on their behalf; in order for Allah to show them mercy and relief from the horrors and terrors of the standing on the Day of Judgment. So he will seek permission to intercede with his Lord and he will be granted permission. And he will assume the station of praise and he will be envied by the first of creation and the last of them. And all of creation will be in gratitude towards him.

May the blessings and peace of Allah be upon our Prophet Muhammad

Translated by Rasheed ibn Estes Barbee

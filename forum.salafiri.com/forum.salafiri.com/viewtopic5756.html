Contradiction of the People of Innovation in the Deen

Bid'ah Alert: Celebrating The Incident of Al-Israa wal Mi'raaj

تناقض :
يحتفلون بليلة الاسراء والمعراج
وينفون علو الله تعالى على خلقه بذاته .
مع أن حادثة المعراج من أدلة أهل السنة والجماعة على العلو

Contradiction:

"They (the people of innovation) celebrate the night of al-Israa Wal Mi'raaj, and they deny Allaah being above His creation, with His Thaat. Moreover, the incident of Al-Mi'raaj is from the proofs of Ahlus sunnah wal jama'ah that Allaah is above His creation."

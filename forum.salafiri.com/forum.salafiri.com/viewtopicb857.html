Shaykh Muhammad Sālih al-Uthaymeen رحمه الله said:

The ruling of kissing the Mushaf
FB_IMG_1488676073690.jpg (22.47 KiB) Viewed 20419 times


Kissing the Mushaf is an innovation. The one who is kissing the Mushaf is doing so because he wants to become closer to Allāh, and it is known that no one can become closer to Allāh except by what Allāh has legislated. Allāh has not legislated kissing what has been written from among His speech.

Fatāwa Nūr alad-Darb 373

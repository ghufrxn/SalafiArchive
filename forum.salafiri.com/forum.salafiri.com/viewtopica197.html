Whoever Makes Allaah Similar To His Creation Has Disbelieved

Posted: Fri Dec 30, 2016 12:53 am
by Shehzad Sattar
Na'eem ibn Hammaad al‐Khuzaa'ee, the Shaykh of al‐Bukhaaree:

"Whoever makes Allaah similar to His creation has disbelieved. Whoever denies anything of what Allaah describes Himself with has disbelieved. And there is no Tashbeeh in what Allaah or His Messenger describes Himself with."

Reported by adh‐Dhahabee in Siyar ʹAlaam an‐Nubalaa (10/610) and he said in (13/299) that its chain of report is Saheeh.

Translated by Aboo az‐Zubayr ‘Abdur Rahmaan Harrison from Shaykh Muhammad ibn Saalih al-Uthaymeen's (rahimahullaah) Sharh of Aqeedatul Hamawiyyah

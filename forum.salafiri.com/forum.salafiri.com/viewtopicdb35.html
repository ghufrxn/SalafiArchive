Did The Prophet Meet The Jinn

Question: Is it authentically reported that the Prophet- Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallm- met the Jinn?

Answer: Yes, it is authentically reported that the Prophet -Sallallahu Alaihi Wasallm- told this to the Companions and showed them their tracks. Read what Ibn Katheer wrote in his Tafsir in his comment on Surah Al-Ahqaf: And remember when We sent to you a party of the Jinn to listen to the recitation of the Qur'an[1]. In Surah Ar-Rahman and Surah Al-Jinn, you will find the answer in detail.

[1] Al-Ahqaf 46:29

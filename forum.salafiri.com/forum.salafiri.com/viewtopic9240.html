One of the famous saying's of ʾUmar Bin al-Khattāb (Radī Allāhū ʾAnhū):

"Beware, be cautious and stay away from using opinions in your religion."

(Jamiʾ Bayān al-ʾIlm Wa Fadhlīhī pg no.1041, narration no.202, Compendium Explaining The Nature Of Knowledge)

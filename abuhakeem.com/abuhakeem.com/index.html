Salafiyyah:

Following the Footsteps of the Salaf

The path of Salafiyah is one that embodies following in the footsteps of our Salaf, the pious predesessors from the Sahabah, the Taabi'een (their students) and the At'baa'ut Taabi'een (their successors). The one who follows this path is on the way of Salafiyah.

Imaam Malik said: “The latter part of this Ummah will not be rectified except by way of that which rectified its earlier part”. Our success as an Ummah is by way of following and studying this methodology of theirs in Belief, dealings and worship, in this is our success.

The Noble Revered Prophet of Islam

The 100 Word Eulogy of the Prophet Muhammad by Emperor Hongwu of China (1368-1398)
Posted by Abu.Iyaad on Sunday, March, 12 2017 and filed under History

The Hundred-word Eulogy (百字讃 bǎizizan) is a 100-character praise of Islam and the Islamic prophet Muhammad written by the Hongwu Emperor of China (r. 1368-1398).

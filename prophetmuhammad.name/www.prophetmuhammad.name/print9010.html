Muhammad: The Semitic Prophet
Posted by Abu.Iyaad on Saturday, March, 07 2015 and filed under Biography
Key topics: Biography

A "Semite" is defined by most dictionaries as a "member of a group of people originally of southwestern Asia that includes Jews and Arabs." The Prophet Muḥammad (peace, mercy and blessings be upon him) was Semitic.

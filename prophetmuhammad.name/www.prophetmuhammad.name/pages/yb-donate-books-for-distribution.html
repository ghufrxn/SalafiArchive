Donate Much Needed Books to City Centre Da'wah Stalls in Major Cities Across the UK

There are now seven City Centre Dawah stalls operating in major cities across the UK including London (East and West), Manchester, Birmingham, Oxford, Cardiff and Leicester. They also travel to many other locations besides these cities. The topic of extremism and terrorism is one of the main areas of focus for them and serves as doorway for inquisitive non-Muslims to learn more about Islam.

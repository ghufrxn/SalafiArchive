]]> Yahya ibn Abi Katheer rahimahullah said, “From amongst the salaf’s duaa was:

‏اَللَّهُمَّ سَلِّمْنـِيْ إِلَى رَمَضَانَ وَسَلِّمْ لِـيْ رَمَضَانَ وَتَسَلَّمْهُ مِنِيْ مُتَقَبَّلاً.

‏Allahumma sallimnee ilaa Ramadan, wa sallim lee Ramadan, wa tasallamhu minnee mutaqabbala

‏ O Allāh, keep me safe until Ramadān, keep Ramadān safe for me, and accept it from me.”

[Latā’if al-Ma’ārif | Page 148 | Al-Imām ibn Rajab al-Hambāli rahimahullah]

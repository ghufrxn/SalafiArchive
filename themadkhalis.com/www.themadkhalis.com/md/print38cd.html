What Is In the Audio From Shaykh al-Albaanee

In a nutshell the Shaykh caught up with Abdullah Azzam who followed an organization-wide Ikhwani boycott against the Shaykh because the Shaykh pointed out the doctrine of Wahdat ul-Wujood in the writings of Sayyid Qutb. Despite having a meeting with Shaykh al-Albaanee in his son-in-law's house in which the Shaykh clearly explained to him that you know full well we do not make Takfir of Sayyid Qutb, and that we do not make Takfir except with the establishment of the hujjah (proof) - Abdullah Azzam went on to publish a series of articles in al-Mujtama' magazine with big bold titles, "Shaykh al-Albaanee Makes Takfir of Sayyid Qutb".

So in the above audio someone brings the attention to the Shaykh of a refutation written by Azzam against him, and the Shaykh says that Azzam already wrote against him in the magazine al-Mujtama'. Then he says about Abdullaah Azzaam:

And he (Abdullah Azzam) is, unfortunately, a dhaalim (oppressor)...

The Shaykh continues to explain that when he met up with Abdullah Azzam again and confronted him as to why he did what he did, Azzam claimed that he had heard some youth in Makkah saying that Shaykh al-Albani made Takfir of Sayyid Qutb and on that basis he did what he did!!

The Shaykh then explains that he took a covenant from Abdullah Azzam that he correct this matter (i.e. his slander against Shaykh al-Albaanee) in the very same magazine by publishing a recantation and clarification,

...and yet he did not do so, may Allah have mercy upon him.

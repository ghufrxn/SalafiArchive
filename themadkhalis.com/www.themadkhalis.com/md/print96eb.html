Alhamdulillaah, due to the efforts of these scholars, these Ikhwani stooges were apprehended and some of them spent a stretch in prison upon the recommendation of Shaykh Abd al-Aziz bin Baz (rahimahullaah). These two terms 'Jaamiyyah' (Jaamis) and 'Madkhaliyyah" (Madkhalis) are used negatively to malign and attack the people of the Sunnah. These people have simply followed the way of the Ahl al-Bid'ah throughout history who attack Ahl al-Sunnah with names they devise themselves in order hinder people from the truth.

Below is a gift to the two Ikhwani stooges, Safar al-Hawali and Salman al-Awdah who were behind the invention of these labels to attack Ahl al-Sunnah, and also to their followers.

Shaykh Salih al-Fawzan: Being Labelled with 'Jaamiyyah' is a Praise and a Tazkiyah

Shaykh Salih al-Fawzaan was asked a question about this label of 'Jaamiyyah' and his response is translated below (audio file here):

السائل: يقول أحسن الله إليكم لي زميل لا يذكر حقوق الحكام وأحاديث السمع والطاعة خوفا من أن يسميه الطلاب بأنه جامي من الفرقة الجامية فهل فعله هذا صحيح علما أنه يقول لا أريد أن يتعرض أحد لعرضي؟

Questioner: He says, may Allaah be benevolent to you - "I have a companion who does not mention the ahaadeeth of the rights of the rulers and the ahaadeeth of hearing and obeying out of fear that the students might label him that he is a 'Jaami' from the sect of the 'Jaamiyyah'." So is this action of his correct with the knowledge that he says "I do not want anyone to attack my honour."

الشيخ: الحمد لله هذه مدحة لمن يسمونهم بالجامية أنهم هم الذين يحثون على السمع والطاعة واتباع سنة الرسول صلى الله عليه وسلم وينهون عن الفرقة ... هذه تزكية لمن يسمونهم بالجامية فأنت استمر على هذا ولا عليك من الألقاب اترك الألقاب اترك التنابز بالألقاب والله أعلم وصلى الله وسلم على نبينا محمد وعلى آله وصحبه وسلم

The Shaykh: All praise is due to Allaah, this is a praise for those whom they label as 'Jaamiyyah' in that they are the ones who encourage hearing and obeying and following the Sunnah of the Messenger (sallallaahu alayhi wasallam) and they prohibit from separation (splitting) ... this is a tazkiyah (commendation) for those whom they label as Jaamiyyah. So you should continue upon this and you should have nothing to do with labels, leave the labels, leave the use of condescending labels and Allah knows best, and may the prayers and salutations be upon Muhammad, his family and his companions.

So we say if commanding the people with the Sunnah in times of tribulations makes one a 'Jaami' then alhamdulillah, we are Jaamiyyah and if defending the honour of Mu'aawiyah, Abu Sufyan, and Hind (radiallaahu anhum) makes one a 'Madkhali' then alhamdulillaah we are Madkhalis! These Ikhwanis intend to revile the Sunnah in reality when they revile those whom they label as 'Jaamiyyah' because the 'Jaamiyyah' simply encourage with the Sunnah. And likewise, these people are lifting the veil (sitr) behind which the Companions lie. Mu'aawiyah is the veil for the Companions, whoever attacks Mu'awiyah has opened the door for the rest of the Companions. They do this by declaring anyone who refutes Sayyid Qutb and defends Mu'aawiyah from being expelled from Islaam as being a 'Madkhali' hating and resenting that this Rafidi poison should be brought out and made known to the Ummah and hating that his books should be abandoned!

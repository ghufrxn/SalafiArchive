Shaykh Saalih al-Fawzaan's Support and Praise of Shaykh Rabee's Refutation of Hasan al-Maalikee
Filed under: Shaykh Rabee
Sunday, September 16 2012 - by Admin
Key topics: Hasan Al-Maalikee
Mail to a Friend • Printer friendly

Introduction

Shaykh Rabee' bin Haadee al-Madkhalee played an instrumental role in exposing the Heretical Ash'aris and Mu'tazilis who brought the poison of fikr into this Ummah, combining it with the methodologies of organizational secrecy such as those of Jamal al-Din al-Afghani al-Baatinee. Just as the Shaykh has an instrumental role in refuting the Raafidah, the Sufees, the Khawaarij, the Modernists and other than them from the variuos opposers to the Book and the Sunnah. He was supported by the other Major Scholars in all the major issues of aqidah and manhaj with respect to which he refuted those heretical innovators, their institutions, parties and organizations. Those scholars include Imaam al-Albaani, Imaam Ibn Baz, Imaam Ibn Uthaymeen, Imaam Muqbil bin Haadee, Shaykh Saalih al-Fawzaan, Shaykh Ahmad al-Najmee, Shaykh Saalih al-Luhaydaan and many others. There are people present today whose hearts are filled with hatred and resentment because their symbolic figureheads were spoken of or because their organizations and institutions which harbour evil partisanship and have oppositions to the Salafi aqidah and methodology were spoken of. And thus every faction has a reason for hatred of this Shaykh because he refuted the Jahmite Ash'aris of Egypt, the Sufees, the Khawarij and Takfiris, the Modernists and Liberalists, the Ikhwanis, the Tablighis and the enemies of the da'wah of Tawhid in general. These people try to spread doubts and misconceptions not unlike the doubts and misconceptions spread by the Jahmiyyah against Shaykh al-Islam Ibn Taymiyyah (rahimahullaah) - like for like (and that is the subject of an entire book). In this article we present another item of evidence indicating that Shaykh Rabee' holds an honourable and praiseworthy position with other Scholars, in addition to the multitudes of other evidences on this website. This is a refutation of the doubt-mongers such as Abdullah al-Farsee, Abu Usaamah Khaleefah, Abu Mussab Wajdi Akkari and many others who use numerous methods varying in their sophistication to either make explicit accusations or to mere insinuations against Shaykh Rabee in particular and those scholars more generally who have played an instrumental role in exposing the many fake claimants to Salafiyyah who have emerged over the last two decades in order to further the cause of al-Ikhwan al-Muslimeen within Salafi audiences.

Shaykh Saalih al-Fawzaan's Praise, Commendation and Support For Shaykh Rabee's Refutation of Hasan al-Maalikee

Introduction of the Allaamah, the Shaykh Saalih bin Fawzaan al-Fawzaan

Bismillaahir-Rahmaanir-Raheem

All praise is due to Allaah and may al-salaat and al-salaam be upon our Prophet Muhammad, his family and his companions.

To proceed: Then I have looked at what has the esteemed Shaykh, Rabee' bin Haadee al-Madkhali wrote in his refutation of the book called, "Muhammad bin Abdul-Wahhaab is a Reformative Caller and not a Prophet" of the one called Hasan bin Farihaan al-Maalikee. That is the book in which he attacked the Imaam Muhammad bin Abdul-Wahhaab making claim (by way of this) to refute his great book, "Kashf al-Shubuhaat". This writer who is put to tribulation did not suffice with this humiliating deed but he further wallowed in his deviation and misguidance, and he attacked the mountains of Islaam with his slander and fabrication. And he tried to criticize what was written by the leading scholars of Islaam and guides of the creation in clarifying the correct authentic creed and refuting the repugnant creeds. So I found that the refutation of Shaykh Rabee' - may Allaah preserve him - is sufficient in its field, good in its method, style, and making the opponent dumbstruck (i.e. being confounded and silenced). So may Allaah reward him with the best of rewards and reward him for what he has stood to perform of aiding the truth and repelling falsehood and its people.

And may Allaah send prayers and salutations upon our Prophet Muhammad, his family and his companions.

Written by: Salih bin Fawzaan bin Abdullah al-Fawzaan. Member of the Committee of Major Scholars on 9/8/1423H.

Notes

Alhamdulillaah, this is another piece in the hundreds of jigsaw pieces through which the true picture begins to slowly emerge and through which the detractors and followers of desires are silenced further and further and their hypocrisy and deception laid bare. These people have shubuhaat (doubts, misconceptions) just like the Jahmiyyah throw shubuhaat against Ibn Taymiyyah and just as the grave-worshippers throw shubuhaat against Ibn Abdul-Wahhaab. When Shaykh Rabee' opposed and refuted that fikr they brought into this Ummah and its figureheads (all Ash'aris!) they began to tarnish him with faults, exactly as Imaam Ibn al-Uthaymeen (rahimahullaah) stated (see here and here)!

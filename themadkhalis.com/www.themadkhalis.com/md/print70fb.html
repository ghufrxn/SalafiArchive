Shaykh al-Fawzan Defends Imaams Ibn Baz and Ibn Uthaymin From the Accusation of Irjaa'
Saturday, December 31 2011 - by Admin
Read more articles at TheMadkhalis.Com

Shaykh Saalih al-Fawzaan defends Imaam's Ibn Baz and Ibn al-Uthaymin (rahimahumallaah) from the slanders of the Qutbiyyah Takfiriyyah that they are Murji'ah. This slander has been made by these Takfiris also against Imaam al-Albaani (rahimahullaah) and the Salafi scholars in general.

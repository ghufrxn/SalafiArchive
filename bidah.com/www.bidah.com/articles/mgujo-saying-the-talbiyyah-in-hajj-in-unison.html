Saying the Talbiyyah in Hajj in Unison
Posted by Abu.Iyaad, Editor in Worship
Topics: Talbiyyah • Hajj
 
Mail To Friend 
 
Printer Friendly

Excerpt: Some pilgrims on Hajj make the talbiyyah (labbaik allahumma labbaik...) in unison. So one of them goes to the front, or is in the middle, or the back, and says the talbiyyah, then they follow him with one voice in unison... Download PDF File.

As for the doubt of the Christians that the Qurʿān is claimed to be uncreated and eternal by Muslims and yet exists "within" God's creation, and hence, this is a proof that Jesus (عليه السلام), who is the Word of God, can be divine whilst also being within the creation. Then this argument rests upon an unorthodox, heretical creed imported into Islām under the influence of Greek philosophy. An umbrella term, "Ahl al-Kalām" refers to numerous sects who spoke about Allāh, His Names, His Attributes, His Speech and the Qurʿān based upon speculation and the baggage of Greek metaphysical language regarding bodies (ajsām) and their incidental attributes, or accidents (aʿrāḍ).

When Did Taqleed Start

When Did Taqleed Start

Shaah Waleeullaah Delhwi said, “know that the people were not agreed upon Taqleed with reference to one particular madhab before the fourth century” (Hujjatullah al-Baaligha pg.157)

Shah Abdul Azeez said “the people were not doing Taqleed of one madhab up until the time of Imaam Maalik” (Baustan al-Muhadditheen pg.17)

Imaam Ibn Qayyim said,”this innovation of Taqleed started in the fourth century and the Messenger of Allaah censured it verbally” (Alaam al-Muwaqqieen 1/222)

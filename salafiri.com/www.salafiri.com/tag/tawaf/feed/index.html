Performing Sa’e between Safa and Marwa

[146] From Malik from Jafar ibn Muhammad from his father from Jabir ibn Abdullah that the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam), walked when he came down from Safa and Marwa and then, when he reached the middle of the valley, he broke into a light run until he had left it.

[GRADING] – [IT’S CHAIN IS SAHIH]

[REFERENCES] 

Al-Muwatta (Yahya’s narration 1/374, 375 hadith no.851, book 20, chapter 42, hadith no.131), at-Tamhid (2/93), al-Istidhkar: (no.799)

Transmitted by Nasa’i (5/243 hadith no.2984) from the hadith of Abdurahman Ibn al-Qasim from Malik

[UNDERSTANDING FROM THE HADITH]
1) An individual should walk between Safa and Marwa when doing Sa’e and if an individual has a sharia compliant excuse such as an illness then it is allowed for that individual to perform Sa’e on a wheelchair, being carried on their companions shoulders or lying down on a bed. 

2) If an individual performing Sa’e assists and carries another person to perform Sa’e for a wage or for free, then the Sa’e of the individual who provides the help and support is valid and this is the same ruling for Tawaf also. 

3) The Sa’e should be performed after performing the Tawaf of the House of Allah therefore doing Tawaf first and then doing the Sa’e is incorrect.

The Tawaf Will Begin From The Hajri Aswad (Black Stone)

[142] From Malik from Jafar ibn Muhammad from his father that Jabir ibn Abdullah (Radi Allahu Anhu) said, “I saw the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) hastening from the Black Stone until he reached it again, three times.”

[GRADING] – [IT’S CHAIN IS SAHIH]

[REFERENCES] – [MUSLIM]

Al-Muwatta (Yahya’s narration 1/364 hadith no.827, book 20, chapter 34, hadith no.107), at-Tamhid (2/68), al-Istidhkar: (no.775)

Transmitted by Muslim (235/1263) from the hadeeth of Maalik

[UNDERSTANDING FROM THE HADITH]
1) There is a consensus that the Tawaf starts from the black stone. Then you go anti-clockwise with the maqam of Ibrahim on your right hand side and the Ka’bah on your left hand side. It is sunnah to run on the first three circuits and then to walk in the remaining four circuits.

2) It is from the sunnah to kiss, touch or say Bismillah Allahu Akbar and points towards the black stone.

3) It is not allowed to perform the tawaf backwards or clockwise.

4) During the farewell Hajj the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) performed Ramal in the first three circuits, meaning that he walked really fast almost like running (brisk walking) and at that time none of the polytheists from Makkah were present therefore Ramal is sunnah until the day of Judgement.

5) There is a consensus that the women will not perform Ramal during the Tawaf rather they will walk normally in all seven circuits of Tawaf.

6) If an individual is not able to perform Ramal then no dam (compensation) is obligatory for them to do and if they are unable to do Ramal because of not having enough free space then there is no sin upon them.

7) The Ramal starts from the black stone and finishes at the black stone (i.e. one full lap). The Ramal (from the black stone) till the Yemeni corner is for the weak only.

 It Is Allowed To Perform Tawaf Whilst Riding

[91] From Maalik from Abul-Aswad Muhammad ibn Abd ur-Rahman ibn Nawfal from Urwa ibn az-Zubair from Zainab bint Abi Salama that Um Salama (Radi Allahu Anha), the wife of the Prophet, (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) said, “I once complained to the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) that I was ill and he said, ‘Do tawaf riding behind the people.’ So I did tawaf riding my camel, while the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) was praying by the side of the House, reciting Surat at-Tur.”

[GRADING] – [IT’S CHAIN IS SAHIH]

[REFERENCES] – [AGREED UPON]

Al-Muwatta (Yahya’s narration 1/370, 371 hadith no.843, book 20, chapter 40, hadith no.123), at-Tamhid (13/99), al-Istidhkar: (no.791)

Transmitted by Bukhari (no.464) and Muslim (no.1276) from the hadith of Malik

[UNDERSTANDING FROM THE HADITH]
1) There is an ijma that those who are ill or have a sharia compliant excuse can perform tawaf of the house of Allah and do Sa’i between Safa and Marwa whilst riding (for e.g. like on a wheelchair) 

2) According to what is most correct, the Prophet (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) was reciting the surah in the Fajr prayer.

3) Just like women are in the row behind the men during salah, in the same manner women should perform the tawaf behind the men and separate from them. However if it is not possible due to difficulty, a necessity or emergency then women can perform tawaf with men. Performing tawaf by staying seperate is allowed just as it is established from the hadith in Sahih Bukhari (no.1618)

4) It is not necessary for women to participate in praying the salah in congregation even if they are in the Masjid.

It is not Allowed to Perform Tawaf Inside the Hatim During the Tawaf

[60] And from the same chain of narration that from Malik from Ibn Shihab from Salim Ibn Abdullah that Abdullah Ibn Muhammad Ibn Abu Bakr as-Sidiq told Abdullah Ibn Umar (RadhiAllahu Anhu) from A’isha (RadhiAllahu Anha), that the Prophet (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) said, “Don’t you see (do you not know) that when your people (the Quraysh of Makah) built the Ka’bah they fell short of the foundations of Ibrahim (Alayhisalam)?” A’isha (RadhiAllahu Anha) said, “Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam), won’t you return it to the foundations of Ibrahim (Alayhisalam)?” and the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) said, “If it were not that your people have only recently left kufr (disbelief) (and become Muslims), I would have done so.”

Salim Ibn Abdullah said that Abdullah Ibn Umar (RadhiAllahu Anhu) said (After he heard this that), “If A’isha (RadhiAllahu Anha) heard this from the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) then I consider that the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) only refrained from greeting the two corners (in tawaf) which are adjacent to the Hijr (Hatim) because the House had not been completed on the foundations of Ibrahim (Alayhisalam).” (i.e. the corners he did not touch were not the original corners of the Ka’bah) .

[GRADING] – [SAHIH]

Ibn Shihab az-Zuhri (did) anana in what I know and he narrated from Nafi’ the mawla of Ibn Umar from Abdullah Ibn Abu Bakr as-Sidiq and the hadith has many routes.

[REFERENCES] – [AGREED UPON]

Al-Muwatta ((Yahya’s narration) 1/363, 364 hadith no.824, book 20, chapter 33, hadith no.104 and with it: he did not complement (lam yutamam)) at-Tamhid (10/26) al-Istidhkar: (no.772)    

Transmitted by Bukhari (no.4484) and Muslim (399/1333) from the hadith of Malik. From Yahya Ibn Yahya’s narration and in the original: Qalaj, mistake from the publisher.

[UNDERSTANDING FROM THE HADITH]

1) If two matters are allowed and are established from the Qur’an and Sunnah, then in order to be safe from evil and corruption one of them does not have to be performed and can be left (temporarily).

2) The Mother of the believers A’isha (RadhiAllahu Anha) said, “I find no difference if I was to pray in the Hijr (Hatim) or pray inside the House of Allah (Ka’bah) ” (al-Muwatta Yahya’s narration 1/364 hadith no.825 and its chain is [Sahih] authentic) Meaning (that) praying inside the Hatim is the same as praying inside the House of Allah.

3) According to the most correct saying, whoever performs tawaf inside the Hatim that person’s tawaf is not valid.

4) Ibn Umar (RadhiAllahu Anhu) not having knowledge of this [Sahih] authentic hadith is clear proof that some Ahadith can be unknown to even the biggest scholars.

5) It is not correct for anyone to think they have complete knowledge of all the evidences of the Shar’iah.

6) There is a difference of opinion regarding who built the House of Allah first. It is established from the Majestic Qur’an that the person who built the foundations of the House of Allah was Ibrahim (Alayhisalam). The Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Wa Sallam) was asked which Masjid was built first? So he replied: Masjid Haram, then it was asked: what Masjid was built after that? So he replied: Masjid Aqsa, then it was asked: how long was it between the construction of these two? So he replied: 40 (years). Sahih Bukhari (hadith (no.3425)) and Sahih Muslim (hadith (no.520))

7) A summarised saying of Ali (RadiAllahu Anhu) is that the people of Nuh (Alayhisalam) and the people of Ibrahim (Alayhisalam) used to live in houses however, the House of Allah is the first place of worship. Al-Mukhtar of al-Dhiya al-Maqdisi (2/60 hadith (no.438)) and al-Mustadrak of al-Hakim (2/292, 293) and its chain is [Hasan] good.

8) People should not be tested without necessity (when there is no need to and it can be avoided).

The Description (Explanation) of the Types of Hajj

[38] And with the same chain of narration that “We set out with the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) in the year of the farewell Hajj and we went into ihram for Umra. Afterwards, the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) said, ‘Whoever has a sacrificial animal with him should go into ihram for Hajj and Umra together, and he should not leave ihram without leaving ihram for both of them at the same time.’ ” She continued “I was menstruating when I got to Makah, so I did not do tawaf of the House or sa’i between Safa and Marwa. I complained to the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) and he said, ‘Undo your hair and comb it and leave the Umra and go back into ihram for the Hajj.’ “She said, “I did so, and when we had completed the Hajj, the Messenger of Allah (Sallalahu Alayhi Was-Sallam) sent me with Abd ar-Rahman ibn Abu Bakr as-Sidiq to at-Tan’im and I performed an Umra and he said, ‘This is in place of your Umra.’ ” “Those who had entered ihram for the Umra did tawaf of the House and sa’i between Safa and Marwa, then left ihram. Then they did another tawaf after returning from Mina for their Hajj, whereas those who entered ihram for the Hajj or combined the Hajj and the Umra, only did one tawaf.”

 

[GRADING] – [SAHIH]

Ibn Shihab (narrates with) anana and he followed Hisham Ibn Urwa in Bukhari (no.1786) and Muslim (115/1211) and Qasim Ibn Muhammad narrates from A’isha (Radi Allahu Anha), see al-Muwatta (Yahya’s narration 1/410,411 hadith no.951 and its chain is authentic)

[REFERENCES] – [AGREED UPON]

Al-Muwatta (Yahya’s narration 1/411,410 hadith no.951, book 20, chapter 74, hadith no.223 and with it (the words): and wamtashiti (and I combed it)) at-Tamhid (8/198), al-Istidhkar: (no.892)     

Transmitted by Bukhari (no.1556) and Muslim (111/1211) from the hadith of Malik.

[UNDERSTANDING FROM THE HADITH]

1) Acting upon the three types of Hajj: Ifrad, Qiran and Tamat’u is completely correct. (at-Tamhid (8/205)) It is established from an authentic hadith of Sahih Muslim (no.1252) that the Hajj Ifrad will continue until the day of judgement. Also see as-Sunan al-Kubra of al-Bayhaqi (5/2) therefore saying that Hajj Ifrad is abrogated [mansukh] is false. Note: It is established from authentic narrations [Ahadith] that the most virtuous type of Hajj from the three types according to the most correct opinion is Hajj Tamat’u.

2) There is only one Tawaf (the tawaf which is done with the House of Allah in the beginning) in the Hajj Qiran and Hajj Ifrad whilst the person doing Hajj Tamat’u has to perform two Tawafs along with Qurbani. The more the actions which are sunnah (that are done) the more reward is given.

3) It is not allowed to perform the tawaf and sa’i in the state of menstruation.

4) There is ijma that the person doing Umra should first do tawaf of the House of Allah then do the sa’i of safa and marwa. See at-Tamhid (8/216) except when the person reaches Makah on the night of Arafah so in this condition he will go to Arafat first so that (a great pillar of) Hajj is not left.

5) The menstruating woman who’s Umra is wajib (obligatory) on her,  can go to Tan’im to perform Umra.

6) Tan’im is a place at Makah Mukaramah where today it is called Masjid A’isha. Some people perform optional Umrah from here whereby there is no proof from the authentic Ahadith and sayings of the salaf salihin. Also see hadith (no.39)

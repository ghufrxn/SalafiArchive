Answering Abul Hasan Hussain Ahmed & Gibril Fouad Haddad Pertaining To The Narration of Abu Ayoob al-Ansaari (RadhiAllahu Anhu) – COMPLETE 4 VOLUMES & UPDATED

Compiled, Translated & Annotated
Abu Hibban & Abu Khuzaimah Ansari
In this book we have discussed
The authenticity of the narration under discussion, A detailed look at the statements of the scholars of hadeeth and rijaal, The gradings of the scholars of hadeeth and rijaal, A detailed look at the terminologies of the science of hadeeth, The confusions and contradictions of the Soofee hanafee’s, The impermissibility of touching the Prophets grave, The real position of Imaam Ahmad on touching the Prophets grave.
The reality of Imaam Haakims authentications and Haafidh Dhahabee agreeing with him, The reality of Imaam Haakim and his gradings, The status of Musnad of Imaam Ahmad, The grading of ‘hasan’ of Imaam Tirmidhee, The Gradings of Dr Muhammad Musafaa al-A’dhamee, The Gradings of Allaamah Muhammad Naasir ud deen al-Albaanee
The reality of Mahmood Sa’eed Mamduh, Eesaa al-Himayree, Muhammad bin Alawee al-Maalikee Soofee, Muhammad Zahid al-Kawtharee, Abdul Fattah Abu Guddah, Muhammad Awwamah and 65 statements of the Scholars on Graves.
(In this treatise we have also answered and refuted, Shaikh Zafar Ahmed Uthmaanee Hanafee, Muhammad Alawee al-Malikee Soofee, Mahmood Sa’eed Mamduh Soofee, the lying Soofee Eesaa al-Himyaree, Abu Layth and others. Similarly we have shown the contradictions, inconsistencies and the incongruity of Abul Hasan Hussain Ahmed and Gibril Fouad Haddad from the ink and words of their own Hanafee elders like Shaikh Zafar Ahmed Uthmanee, Shaikh Abdul Fattah Abu Guddah, Shaikh Abdul Hayy Lucknowee, Shaikh Shu’ayb al-Arnaa’oot and their underlings pertaining to Hadeeth and it Sciences. Furthermore, we have shown this from books that have been verified and checked by Abul Hasan Hussain Ahmed and Gibril Fouad Haddad’s teachers, those they have alleged ‘ijazahs’ from and those who are their trusted authorities, from the likes of Shaikh Noor ud deen Ittar, Muhammad Awaamah, Shaikh Shu’ayb al-Arnaa’oot and so on)

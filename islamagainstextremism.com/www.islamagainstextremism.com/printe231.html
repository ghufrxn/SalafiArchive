Saudi Interior Minister: Some States Sponsor Terrorism to Target Kingdom
Posted by Admin on Wednesday, March, 11 2015 and filed under News

Saudi Deputy Crown Prince and Interior Minister Mohammad bin Naif bin Abdulaziz on Tuesday accused unnamed states of sponsoring terrorist groups to target Saudi Arabia.Prince bin Naif, who is also Second Deputy Premier, made the statements during 32nd Meeting of the Arab Interior Ministers Council in Algeria.

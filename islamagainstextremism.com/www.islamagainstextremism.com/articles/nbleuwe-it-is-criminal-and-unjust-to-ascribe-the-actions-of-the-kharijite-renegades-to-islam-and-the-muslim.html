It Is Criminal and Unjust to Ascribe the Actions of the Kharijite Renegades (Al-Qaidah, ISIS) to Islam and the Muslims
Posted by Admin, Senior Editor in Articles
 
Mail To Friend 
 
Printer Friendly

It is Criminal and Unjust to Ascribe the Actions of the Khārijite Renegades such as al-Qāʾidah and ISIS to Islām and the Muslims. As occurs in an abundance of authentically transmitted narrations from the Prophet Muḥammad (sallallāhu ʾalayhi wasallam), he informed of the imminent and recurring appearance - after his death - of a group known as the Khārijites (extremist renegades) who would depart from Islām and the main body of Muslims, excommunicate them, fight against them and kill them. He mentioned many of their evil traits and encouraged that they be sought and killed until they are rooted out. Download Leaflet in PDF (Print and distribute!).

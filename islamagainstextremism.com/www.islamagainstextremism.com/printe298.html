Has Peaceful Preaching and Good Argumentation Been Abrogated and Was Islam Spread by the Sword?
Posted by Admin on Wednesday, June, 28 2017 and filed under Articles

A common misconception is that the verses of the Qur'an that permit or enjoin fighting have abrogated all the verses that enjoin peaceful, patient preaching and good argumentation. This is an erroneous viewpoint and is shared unfortunately by misinformed Muslims and non-Muslims alike. There are many arguments to refute this view and we shall present more in other articles in the future inshaa'Allaah.

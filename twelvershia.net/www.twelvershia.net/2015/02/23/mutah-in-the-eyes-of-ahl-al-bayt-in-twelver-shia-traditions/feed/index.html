Comments:-
وَالْمُحْصَنَاتُ مِنَ النِّسَاءِ إِلَّا مَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَانُكُمْ كِتَابَ اللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ
And from the Halaal/allowable women; those women are also not allowed for you to marry (who are already married except the new Muslim women (1) married women(who came under your right hand/custody after becoming Muslim).(You are to examine them 60/10 so that it may become the duty of the Muslim society to provide them their marital status).Allah has made this your duty.
وَأُحِلَّ لَكُم مَّا وَرَاءَ ذَٰلِكُمْ أَن تَبْتَغُوا بِأَمْوَالِكُم مُّحْصِنِينَ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحِينَ فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُم بِهِ مِنْهُنَّ فَآتُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ فَرِيضَةً وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِيمَا تَرَاضَيْتُم بِهِ مِن بَعْدِ الْفَرِيضَةِ إِنَّ اللَّهَ كَانَ عَلِيمًا حَكِيمًا
Except the above mentioned women all the other are allowed for you(Conditionally)hem; if you demand for Nikkah /marriage with your wealth and the purpose of Nikkah will be ; to remain in Nikkah for the whole life long but not only to satisfy your lust(not to only to satisfy your sexual satisfaction/demand.). Then you can benefit the Nikkah from those women after paying their MEHR/Dowry at the time of Nikkah. There is no harm if you become agreed after fixing the Mehr/Dowry(Your wives return some of the dowry amount return back to you with their happiness)(1).Allah’s laws are based on upon knowledge and wisdom.
(1) فِيمَا تَرَاضَيْتُم بِهِ مِن بَعْدِ الْفَرِيضَةِ these words has come after فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُم بِهِ مِنْهُنَّ فَآتُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ فَرِيضَةً in which some of the amount from the fixed dowry has been allowable to get back; after mutual understanding/agreement. Here فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُم shows the durable Nikkah/marriage but never the so called temporary marriage (MUTTAمْتَعْ) can be proved. Because when a woman marries and gets dowry for one day who is to liberate next day from that marriage automatically ; will never have the real love with one day husband to return back some of the dowry amount back to this husband.
It is to be clarified that one group of the Muslim conceives from the فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُم بِهِ مِنْهُنَّ aboutمْتَعْ so called Nikkah which can never be assumed from this as temporary marriage ,Because One day wife will never be agreed/ready to return the one day husband not even a penny.
So in these words of فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُم بِهِ مِنْهُنَّ the meaning of مْتَعْ can never be conceived and is totally wrong.

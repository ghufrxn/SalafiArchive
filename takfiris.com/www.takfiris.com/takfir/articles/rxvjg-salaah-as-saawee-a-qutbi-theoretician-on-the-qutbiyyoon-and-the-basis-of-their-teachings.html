Salaah as-Saawee (A Qutbi Theoretician) on the Qutbiyyoon and the Basis of Their Teachings
Filed under: Articles
Wednesday, December 30 2009 - by Takfiris.Com
Key topics: Salaah As-Saawee • Salah As-Sawi • Qutbiyyah • Qutbis

Mail to a Friend • Printer friendly

Here's a little reminder from Salaah as-Saawee, one of the ideologists of the Qutbiyyah today, and who has many works laying down the methodologies for these people. He says in "Madaa Shar'iyat il-Intimaa ilal Ahzaab wal-Jamaa'aat al-Islaamiyyah" (p.171):

أما القطبيون فقد قام منهجهم ابتداءً على بلورة قضية التشريع , وبيان صلتها بالدين , وبيان أن الخلل الذي يغشى أنظمة الحكم في مجتمعاتنا المعاصرة ناقض لعقد الإسلام , وهادم لأصل التوحيد , ومعلوم أن الكتب التي تمثل هذا الاتجاه وتعبر عنه منهجه هي كتب سيد قطب - رحمه لله في مجال الدعوة والمخاطبة العامة

As for the Qutubiyyoon (the Qutbis), then their methodology (manhaj) is based primarily upon the issue of legislation (tashree'), explaining its link with the foundation of the religion and explaining that the various flaws that have engulfed the legal structures in our contemporary societies nullify the contract of Islaam and demolish the very basis of Tawheed. It is also known that the books that represent this particular orientation and which outline its methodology are the books of the teacher, Sayyid Qutb - may Allaah have mercy upon him - in the field of da'wah and public speeches.

We have established proof now from the mufakkireen themselves - (see here from al-Qaradawi) - of the existence of a group known as "the Qutbiyyah". The likes of Shaykh Ibn Uthaymeen and Shaykh Saalih al-Fawzaan (see here, here), and other Scholars have spoken of this revolutionary ideology spawned by Qutb and his works. This proves that this is not an invention of the so-called "Madkhalis". This label ("Madkhalis") is used to divert people away from the truth and from the true Scholars, by a people whose ship was sunk and who have been left hanging onto the flotsam for the past 10 years.

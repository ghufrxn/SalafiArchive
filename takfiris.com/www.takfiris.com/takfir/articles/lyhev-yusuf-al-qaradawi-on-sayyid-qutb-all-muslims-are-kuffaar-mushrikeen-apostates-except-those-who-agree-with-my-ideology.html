Yusuf al-Qaradawi on Sayyid Qutb: All Muslims Are Apostates (Kuffaar, Mushrikeen) Except Those Who Agree With My Ideology and Join My Group
Filed under: Articles
Thursday, December 31 2009 - by Takfiris.Com
Key topics: Yusuf Al-Qaradawi • Qutbiyyah • Takfiri Movements • Qutbis

Mail to a Friend • Printer friendly

Yusuf al-Qardawi is one of the most prominent of the contemporary figureheads of al-Ikhwan al-Muslimeen and he has written many times on the movements of Takfir. We see a couple of trends amongst todays Ikhwanis. Seeing that the ideologies of Takfir found in Qutb's books are harmful to their cause, a group amongst them (like al-Qaradawi) are trying to distance the Ikhwaan from these teachings. He is opposed by others, who try to downplay the issue of Takfir and argue that the books of Sayyid Qutb have been fundamental to the Ikhwaani movement, and that they have an important role to play. We'll leave them to argue about these issues, but here is a statement of al-Qaradawi taken from an article published on 4th May 2004 called (هل يكفّر "سيد قطب" مسلمي اليوم؟) in which he outlines the ideology of Takfir in the works of Sayyid Qutb.

View the entire article here in Arabic

He writes, after quoting a passage from Qutb's commentary az-Zilal (7/269) (في ظلال القرآن طبعة عيسى الحلبي الثانية), and he is essentially summarizing the main points from what he quoted from Qutb:

النص واضح تمام الوضوح: إن الكاتب لا يعتبر مسلما إلا من آمن بفكرته هذه، وهي الفئة التي يسميها "العصبة المسلمة"، وهي التي يجب أن تشعر بأنها وحدها هي "الأمة المسلمة"، وأن ما حولها ومن حولها ممن لم يدخلوا فيما دخلت فيه جاهلية وأهل جاهلية، أي هم مشركون وكفار، ليس لهم في الإسلام نصيب، وإن كانوا يصلون ويصومون ويزكون ويحجون! فكأنما المسلمون جميعا بمثابة مشركي مكة عند البعثة المحمدية، وكأنما دعوته بمثابة دعوة محمد صلى الله عليه وسلم؛ من آمن بها دخل في الإسلام، ومن لم يؤمن بها فهو جاهلي كافر حلال الدم!!

The text is clear, and completely apparent: The writer (Qutb) does not consider [anyone] to be a Muslim except whoever believes in this ideology of his, and this is the group that he calls the "Muslim Vanguard", and this [group] is the one for which it is obligatory to realize that it alone is the "Muslim Ummah", and that whatever is around it and whoever is around it, from those who have not entered into what [this group] has entered into, is [itself] Jaahiliyyah and [those people are] the people of Jaahiliyyah, meaning they are Mushriks and Kuffar, they do not have any share of Islaam, even if they pray, fast, give zakah, and make the pilgrimage. It is as if all the Muslims are equivalent to the Mushriks of Makkah at the time of the sending of Muhammad (as a Messenger), and it is as if his da'wah is equivalent to the da'wah of Muhammad (sallallaahu alayhi wasallam), whoever believed in that da'wah entered into Islaam and whoever did not believe in it, then he is a Jaahiliyy, Kaafir, whose blood is permissible to shed!!

There is no doubt to any sincere Muslim, that this is the deen of the Khawaarij, only it is more severe, more evil and more repugnant, and amazing is the state of a people willing to expend the greatest of efforts in trying to propagandize for this man's works and to defend him and his books in which there is nothing left, after you remove all the aspects of the aqidah of the Jahmiyyah, Mu'tazilah, Ash'ariyyah, Raafidah, except the madhhab of the Khawaarij and the manifestos of secular atheist Jews, such as Vladimir Lenin.

This is the same evil methodology we find today. The contemporary followers of this Ash'arite reviver of the deen of the Khawaarij make Takfir of all Muslim countries, their rulers and governments and then anyone who does not agree with their Takfir, or their advocated methodology brought to them by Qutb from the manifestos of secular atheist Jews, is considered an enemy of Islam and the Muslims. That's the irony of the whole thing. It is these people, upon these doctrines, and their remnants amongst whom the Dajjaal (Anti-Christ) will appear (see here). These people, in their delusion, think that playing a bit of "cyber-butch" (pronouncing Takfir, attacking the rulers and scholars in forums and blogs online) amongst their peers is "enjoining the good and forbidding the evil" and "jihad in the path of Allaah". Meanwhile they continue living in the secular lands that they have given preference to and in which they find great ease, comfort and luxury. Not long ago they were claiming that the Maturidi Hanafi Taliban have established the Islamic State! We did not see these people making hijrah over there in hoards and masses.

Meanwhile, they [appear to] lament over Afghanistan and Iraq, resorting to Secularism and secularist methodologies in their protests and demonstrations and activities. They play and act all "cyber-butch" whilst writing disclaimers on their blogs and forums (with the meaning), "Hey, we free ourselves from what anyone writes on our forums that goes against British and US Laws, and we abide by the British, or US laws (please, please Bush and Blair, please don't throw us in jail for what other people write on our forums, we're sticking to your Qawaaneen and we won't tolerate violation of your Qawaaneen on our forums!)".

Ironically on the same blogs and forums these deluded clowns and shysters publicly extol the virtues of speaking the truth in front of a tyrannical ruler. What cowards! Their forums are based and operate upon secularist principles, they allow every naa'iq (secularist, liberalist, atheist, jahmi, ash'ari, soofee, mu'tazili, aqlaani etc.) a platform, and they institute Qawaaneen for the operation of these forums based upon secular principles, and invite others to implement these Qawaaneen. Their disclaimer above was on their forum for many long years and as soon as this article was posted, they took it down.

Everything about these people is a lie. Its all hypocrisy. Its a sham and a fraud. And deep inside their souls they know it!

They come out in the name of caring for the Muslim Ummah, the loftiest of slogans, but it does not concern them that the evil ideologies they are upon played instrumental roles in the murder of tens of thousands of innocent Muslims (take Algeria as an example in the 1990s or Syria in 1982), let alone being a prime factor in the continued justification of the ghazwah of the Muslim lands. Not for one day have these dogs aided Islaam and the Muslims, and nor have they brought any mercy or compassion to the average non-Muslim through any real da'wah to the Tawheed of the Messengers. This is why in the whole of history, never has Allaah allowed the Kharijite dogs, holding such repugnant beliefs, to establish a dawlah, they never had a dawlah, and Allaah willing, they never will - for every time they emerge they will always be cut off, as is stated in the Prophetic Hadeeth about them. Everyone is a kaafir and so are you unless you believe in my ideology and join me in my secular atheist Leninist methodology to snatch power from the apostates

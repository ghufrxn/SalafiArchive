And those who have with them what contradicts what the Messengers informed of are of three groups:

The people of takhyeel (imagination): From the Heretical Philosophers and the Baatiniiyyah, those who say that the Messengers informed about the affair of faith in Allah and the Last Day with what opposes the actual reality so that they may cause the masses to imagine (unreal) things which would benefit them (in the worldd) and they consider this to be from the virtues of the Messsengers, and I have refuted those in detail in other than this place.

The Permanent Committee of Scholars on Ibn Taymiyyah, Ahl al-Kalām and Tasalsul (Endless Chain of Events)
Thursday, August 20 2015 - by Abu.Iyaad
Read more articles at Aqidah.Com

The Permanent Committee for Research and Verdicts clarify that affirming the eternality of Allah's speech and action (through His will and power) does not amount to affirming something eternal alongside Allah and they exonerate Ibn Taymiyyah from this saying.

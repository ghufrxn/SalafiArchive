Sunday, September 06 2015 - by Abu.Iyaad

Due to the efforts of many Muslim apologists, the notion that the Qurʾān alludes to or explicitly mentions the speculative, conjectural pseudo-scientific philosophical big bang model of the universe is now commonly used by average Muslims to impress non-Muslims about the Divine source of the Qurʾān. The reason why this appeals to Muslim apologists is because the big bang model suggests an absolute beginning for the universe even though many of its atheist proponents deny this is the case at all.

The claim that the Salaf or these Kullabi Ash'aris were upon tafwid in this matter is known to be spurious through the fact that they rebutted the ta'wils of the Jahmiyyah and Mu'tazilah and also denied the charge of tajsim and tashbih. This proves that the contention was one of meaning, proving the Salaf and the Kullabi Ash'aris affirmed the meanings, and their denial was in relation to having knowledge of the kaifiyyah. This is very apparent from reading the articles listed above.

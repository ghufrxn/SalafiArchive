Prophet Muhammad (Sallallahu ‘Alayhi Wa Sallam) said to his daughter Fatima (RadiAllahu ‘Anha) : “Fa innee ni’mas Salafu ana laki (How excellent a Salaf I am for you !).” (Bukhari, H/6286; Muslim, H/2450)
‘Salafi’ means one who follows the Quran and the Hadith as understood and practised by the Salaf us-Saalih (Pious Predecessors).
Salafi is an ascription for a true Muslim to distinguish him from innovators.
